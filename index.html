POPULAR DESTINATIONS

Lapland

Step into a winter wonderland and discover the magic of Lapland, Finland’s northernmost region. Famous for its stunning snow-covered landscapes, enchanting Northern Lights, and the home of Santa Claus, Lapland is a destination like no other. Whether you're seeking thrilling adventures like husky sledding and snowmobiling or prefer the quiet beauty of reindeer sleigh rides and serene Arctic wilderness, Lapland has it all. Embrace the cozy warmth of Finnish saunas, stay in unique glass igloos, and immerse yourself in the rich traditions of the Sámi people. Experience the true essence of winter and the magic of Lapland!

Aurora Sight

Witness the breathtaking beauty of the Northern Lights, one of nature's most awe-inspiring phenomena. Aurora Sight offers you the chance to experience the magical dance of colors in the Arctic skies. Whether viewed from a cozy glass igloo, an open wilderness camp, or while on a thrilling night safari, the Aurora Borealis is a sight you'll never forget. Learn about the science and myths behind this natural wonder as you create memories that last a lifetime. Let us guide you to the best locations and moments for a front-row seat to this dazzling spectacle.

Vepsa Island

Escape to the tranquil beauty of Vepsä Island, a hidden gem in the Turku Archipelago. Known for its serene atmosphere and stunning natural surroundings, Vepsä is the perfect getaway for those seeking relaxation or adventure. Enjoy sandy beaches, scenic hiking trails, and refreshing swims in crystal-clear waters. The island also offers cozy cabins, picnic spots, and opportunities for kayaking or paddleboarding.Whether you're planning a family outing, a romantic retreat, or a day trip with friends, Vepsä Island provides a peaceful escape where nature takes center stage.
