Vepsä Island

Escape to the tranquil beauty of Vepsä Island, a hidden gem in the Turku Archipelago. Known for its serene atmosphere and stunning natural surroundings, Vepsä is the perfect getaway for those seeking relaxation or adventure. Enjoy sandy beaches, scenic hiking trails, and refreshing swims in crystal-clear waters. The island also offers cozy cabins, picnic spots, and opportunities for kayaking or paddleboarding. Whether you're planning a family outing, a romantic retreat, or a day trip with friends, Vepsä Island provides a peaceful escape where nature takes center stage.
